Workshops



Bluetooth Control

Bluetooth works by the simple principle of sending and receiving data in the form of radio waves. Every Bluetooth enabled device has a card-like attachment known as the Bluetooth adapter. It is this Bluetooth adapter that sends and receives data. We made a Bluetooth controlled robot car which was easy to operate using a mobile application. It’s simple in making and easy to understand. Its the kind of basics of robotics that every student above certain age must know.

Obstacle Avoiding

Obstacle avoiding is a concept used in the tesla self driving car which detects any human, vehicle or any obstacle in its proximity and changes the path of the vehicle to avoid collision. This concept is one of the defining pillars of the self driving car.

Line Following Bot

Line following is based on the automated concept of following a path, using IR sensors. This is also one of the technology used in Tesla cars. Where the vehicle follows the particular path it is dedicated to. The code of the program plays an important role here.

Projects

FDM

Fused deposition modeling, or FDM 3D Printing, is a method of additive manufacturing where layers of materials are fused together in a pattern to create an object. The material is usually melted just past its glass transition temperature, and then extruded in a pattern next to or on top of previous extrusions, creating an object layer by layer.

Robotic Arm

Robotic arm is a 3 degree of freedom with a claw grip at its head. it serves huge industrial purposes in assembling in automobile industry, assisting in medical industry. it is also used in International Space Station. robotic arm is capable of performing extremely difficult tasks in very small period of time. it runs on arduino and controlled by ROS.

Mars Rover

Mars Rover is a belt run bot designed to work in all conditions. be it craters of moon or plateaus of mars. the product we made is a small scale replica of what can be a fully functional atv which can be operated without human presence.


Drone

A four propeller drone with the idea of Aerial security, this a Four propeller drone with brushless dc motors.Range of this drone is 50 feet, with a FSI6 transmitter, made on Arduino. Drone serves many purposes from security, to delivery of products, without the use of manual pilot. Drone also has a lot of potential in the defense and military sector, We have a team working on the R&D of this team , to navigate the further possibilities in the world of aviation

War Bot

This Robotic Vechile took 200 plus hours and a team of 20 people for manufacturing. This Robot is used in High Grade Robotic Competitions, held at a national level, it is capable of Rotating The 25 KG Drum present at the front, at the speed of 1000 RPM, Which is used as weapon.
